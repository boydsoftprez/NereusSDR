mi0bot/Thetis-HL2 — Hermes-Lite 2 fork of Thetis (upstream of NereusSDR HL2 ports)

Portions of NereusSDR (notably Hermes-Lite 2 discovery, IpAddressFixedHL2
handling, EeConfigHL2 fields, and board-id 6 → HermesLite mapping in
`src/core/RadioDiscovery.cpp` and `src/core/P1RadioConnection.cpp`) are
ported from the mi0bot/Thetis-HL2 fork of Thetis, which adds HL2-specific
capability blocks that are not present in the upstream ramdor/Thetis tree.

Upstream repository:
  https://github.com/mi0bot/Thetis-HL2

fork — see `thetis.txt`.)

Per-file mapping between NereusSDR sources and mi0bot/Thetis-HL2 upstream
lives in `docs/attribution/THETIS-PROVENANCE.md` under the heading
"Files derived from mi0bot/Thetis-HL2". The verbatim upstream file header
from the mi0bot fork is preserved at the top of every ported source file.

Corresponding source for this binary is published at the tagged release
on GitHub; see `SOURCE-OFFER.txt`.
