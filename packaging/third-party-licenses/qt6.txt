Qt is licensed under multiple licences including LGPL-3.0 and commercial
terms. NereusSDR links Qt 6 dynamically under LGPL-3.0.

The full LGPLv3 text ships alongside this notice as `LGPLv3.txt`. LGPLv3
is defined by reference to GPLv3 (LGPLv3 §0), so the full GPLv3 text is
also shipped as `GPLv3.txt`. The upstream canonical texts are at:
  https://www.gnu.org/licenses/lgpl-3.0.txt
  https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0.txt

Because NereusSDR links Qt 6 dynamically, the recipient may replace any
Qt shared library on their system with a modified version and re-run
the application — this is the "suitable shared library mechanism" Qt's
LGPLv3 election requires (LGPLv3 §4(d)(0)).

the shared libraries, additional per-module licence texts may be
present there — those supplement, not replace, this notice.
