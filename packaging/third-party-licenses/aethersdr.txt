AetherSDR — Qt6 SDR console (architectural template for NereusSDR)

NereusSDR uses AetherSDR (ten9876/AetherSDR) as its Qt6 architectural
template — class layout, signals/slots shape, floating-applet mechanics,
GPU-pipeline scaffolding, AppSettings XML skeleton, and worker-thread
patterns. Approximately 45 NereusSDR source files derive genuinely from
AetherSDR (Bucket A in `docs/attribution/aethersdr-reconciliation.md`);
the remainder use Thetis for logic and AetherSDR only as an idiomatic
reference.
